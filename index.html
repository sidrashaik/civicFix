How it Works

Citizens Complaint

A registered citizen submits a complaint with a photo and location within 5 km of their address..

Authority Action

The concerned authority views the complaint, fixes the issue, and uploads a “work done” photo.

Closure & Reminder

The citizen confirms to close the complaint. If not resolved on time, the system sends reminders to the authority.
